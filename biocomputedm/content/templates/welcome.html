Biocompute-DM's power is directly enhanced using custom bioinformatics pipelines, details on which can be found in the available pipelines tab (in your activity window when logged in).

Biocompute-DM also offers bioinformaticians a unique platform to facilitate productionising and deploying their pipelines.
